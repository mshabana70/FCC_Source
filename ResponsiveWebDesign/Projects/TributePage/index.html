Lord Beerus

The Deity of Destruction

Introduction:

Beerus (ビルス Birusu) is the God of Destruction of Universe 7. He is accompanied by his martial arts teacher and attendant, Whis. Beerus' twin brother is Champa, the God of Destruction of Universe 6.

Beerus is the main antagonist of the Dragon Ball Z: Battle of Gods film and the God of Destruction Beerus Saga but becomes a supporting character in later sagas.

Physical Abilities:

  • Flight - The ability to fly with the use of ki.
  • Ki' Blast - he most basic form of energy wave. Beerus can fire ki blasts strong enough to destroy planets, as shown in Dragon Ball Heroes.
  • Normal Ki Immunity - In Battle of Gods, Beerus claims that normal ki has absolutely no effect on a deity like himself.
  • Kiai - An invisible wave of ki that Beerus emits from his palm. Used against Mr. Buu.
  • Tail Attack - Beerus attacks the opponent by hitting them with his tail, used against Super Saiyan God Goku when the Saiyan had him in a hold.
